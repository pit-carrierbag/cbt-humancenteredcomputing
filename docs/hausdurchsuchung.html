A political game created in the course of a university project, dealing with the house search at the Austrian Minister of Finance's home. The game is a checkerboard-based, turn-based strategy game in which you try to collect all media containing incriminating evidence in a stroller. Your opponent (the WKStA) is doing the same (except for the stroller). You also have smoke grenades at your disposal to get out of especially precarious situations.

Reception

The game reached about 2.000 installations in the Google Playstore and was reported on in Austrian newspapers such as der Standard or ZackZack . This even resulted in a party member threatening to sue us – a threat unfortunately never realised.
